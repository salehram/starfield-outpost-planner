Starfield Outpost Planner

Welcome to Starfield Outpost Planner!

This tool helps you plan the resources and modules needed for your outpost in the vast reaches of space.

With this hopefully intuitive interface, you can quickly determine resource requirements, calculate total mass, and save your builds for future reference.

The goal of this tool is to just provide a mean for you to know the resource and weight requirements for an outpost module or full build, however this can be scaled to virtually every single manufactured resource or mod in teh game, which probably is worth doing very soon!
